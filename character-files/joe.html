Character Gallery - Joe

Joe plays the trombone like any skeleton. He hung out with the Connoisseur in elementary school (yes, he is the imaginary friend). Despite spending time together, they barely talked; the Connoisseur can only recall two conversations with him throughout his whole life. When Joe moved away, the Connoisseur didn't even notice his absence until he revisited those conversations in his mind. Anyhow, Joe suddenly reappeared out of nowhere, and the Connoisseur is glad to have him back.
