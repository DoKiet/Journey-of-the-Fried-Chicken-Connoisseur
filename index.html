This website was created on a whim as an attempt to rant about boredom. Boredom has been a close friend of mine, but recently, he has become too much to bear. My conscience has irritated him. I wish to apologize but playing adult in a childlike dimension has refrain me from doing so.

Artist's Statement

Originally, I had this whole box filled with just the word yap. After a long time now and revisiting my website, I realized how incredibly cringy I am. Of course, I still plan to keep all original works up since I believe that cringey makes it worthwhile. But holy shit, what was I thinking?

-Fried Chicken Connoisseur

Tim Kreider

Found him when I was sitting in my English class, and my professor provided me with sources to construct my argument essay. Among the sources was "A Busy Trap," which I found hilarious. Later on, I checked out his comic "The Pain -- When Will It End," great stuff, by the way. Now I'm currently collecting his books. To show my support, I celebrate the month of February, not because of various holidays like Valentine's, but because that's the month when FedEx bought out Kinko's Copies (got rid of his early 2000s comics).
